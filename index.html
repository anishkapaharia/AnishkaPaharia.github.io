Under Review, KUSET Journal, 2026.

This work investigates machine learning methods for predicting diabetes using healthcare datasets. We compare multiple predictive models including logistic regression, random forests, and gradient boosting approaches to identify the most effective methods for diabetes risk prediction.

Algorithmic Disparities in Data-Driven Decision Systems: Empirical Evaluation of Group-Level Error and Calibration Differences

Preprints.org, 2026

This work conducted research on algorithmic fairness, model calibration, and ethical AI through empirical analysis of demographic bias and predictive performance in data-driven systems. We evaluate group-level error disparities and calibration differences across different demographic groups to understand and address fairness concerns in machine learning.
